I'm excited to share that I have been promoted to **Senior Software Tech Lead for AD/ADAS Scene Understanding** at Ampère (Renault Group).

In this new role, I will lead the technical direction of the scene understanding stack — perception, semantic interpretation of the driving environment, and the algorithms that allow our vehicles to make sense of the road around them. I look forward to growing the team, shaping the architecture, and pushing forward the state of the art that powers safer, smarter Renault vehicles.

A huge thank you to my colleagues, mentors, and collaborators who made this milestone possible.
